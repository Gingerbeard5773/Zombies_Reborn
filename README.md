# Zombies Fortress Reborn

**Zombies Fortress Reborn** is a Zombie Fortress mod for **[King Arthur's Gold](https://github.com/transhumandesign/kag-base)**, focused on survival, base building, progression, and massive undead hordes.

Build a mighty fortress alone or alongside your friends and defend it against relentless waves of zombies. Every night brings new threats as the undead grow in number and strength, testing both your defenses and your resolve.

Construct deadly traps, wield a wide arsenal of weapons, or recruit and command an army to fight on your behalf. Research powerful technologies and unlock new capabilities to strengthen your fortress, expand your influence, and survive against seemingly impossible odds.
